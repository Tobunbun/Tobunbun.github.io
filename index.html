Vector Illustrations, What are they?

Vector illustrations are artworks that are made up of vector graphics.
When you scale a vector image file, it isn't low resolution and there's
no loss of quality, so it can be sized to however large or small you
need it to be.

Adobe Illustrstor

Adobe Illustrator is vector based graphics software that lets you
scale down your artwork for mobile screens or scale up to a
billboard size.
